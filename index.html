FOR YOUR SWEET TOOTH

Doughy Delights is a new bakery located in Boston. We are dedicated to serving sweet to the tooth treats with favorites including tiramasu, croissants, and even cake pops!

\

Jane Smith

Jane Smith's career as a baker began when she was turning 8. Her passion for art and food came together as she wrote hundreds of recipes down in a cookbook. She attended culinary school upon graduating high school.

Now, Jane is the proud owner of Doughy Delights and is ecstatic to bring her sweet treats to Boston residents. She loves the bustling yet serene location of the Seaport District. Feel free to stop by to say hello to her in the kitchen!
